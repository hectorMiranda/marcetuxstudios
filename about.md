I'm **marcetux** — Hector to most people. Software engineer in Los Angeles by way
of Xalapa, Mexico, with stops in Canada, Switzerland, and Turkey along the road.

I build web platforms and APIs for a living — lately a lot of C#/.NET, REST, and
the unglamorous plumbing that keeps high-traffic sites reporting accurate numbers.
On weekends I'm usually at the bench with an Arduino, a multimeter, and more
breadboards than any reasonable person needs.

This blog is where I write things down so I stop relearning them. Expect posts on
APIs, caching, deployment, the web platform, and electronics.

You can find my code at [github.com/hectorMiranda](https://github.com/hectorMiranda).
