December landed two things to write about — Mistral 7B and structured output
reliability — that are about as concrete and operational as blog posts get. The
reading retrospective and the year retrospective are the personal-stakes ones.
The combination is about right for a month that's half holidays.

The consulting work is winding down as planned. The retrieval pipeline is instrumented
properly now — the observability panel I added in November has already caught two
retrieval degradation events before they became user complaints — and the client has
enough documentation to maintain it without me. Good exits are underrated.

The Raspberry Pi 5 is running Mistral 7B on the shelf and I query it most evenings.
The active cooling fan is always on and I've made peace with the noise. 2024: I want
to write less about setup and more about what you build on top of the setup. The
infrastructure year is done. The application year starts in January.
