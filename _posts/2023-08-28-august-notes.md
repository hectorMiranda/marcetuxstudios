August was the month the pivot became explicit. I wrote it down on the 15th because
writing it down is how I make a decision final — as long as it's in my head it can
be second-guessed, and I've already done enough of that. The Rust work continues and
I'm genuinely proud of the gRPC streaming client and the contract auditing I did this
quarter. The work was good. The company context is harder.

The LLM side of August was about understanding the infrastructure pieces more clearly:
fine-tuning vs RAG as a real design decision rather than a buzzword comparison, and
streaming responses as an actual implementation problem. Both of those came from trying
to answer questions from people who were building things. The teaching forces you to
know the thing precisely.

September is job search month in parallel with whatever the job still needs from me.
The portfolio of evening projects — the RAG pipelines, the Llama 2 local experiments,
the pgvector work — is the thing I'll be talking about. The skill I developed at
CasperLabs is the thing I'll bring with me.
