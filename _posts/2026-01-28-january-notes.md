January was a compression of several things at once — new job, operational studio, and the realization that after a year of freelancing, having a team again has a gravity I had forgotten about. The things you can build with another person who cares about the same problem are qualitatively different from what you build alone, even when alone you're productive.

The identity work at AmaWaterways is genuinely interesting. SCIM, JWT design, Auth0 pipeline — these are topics I've touched before, but the context of a company where the identity domain is genuinely complex (guests, agents, crew, B2B travel sellers, all in one tenant) makes the decisions harder and therefore more instructive. The gap between "I understand this standard" and "I can implement it so that it fails gracefully under the real conditions of a real system" is always bigger than it looks from the outside.

The studio is quieter. I'm mostly setting things up, running cables, finding out which storage decisions I'll regret. But the bench is running and the home lab is serving its traffic and I already know the space is going to be the thing that lets me sustain the side work for another decade. February: deeper into the SCIM idempotency fixes, and I want to finally write up the OpenTelemetry setup properly.
