Month one of 2013 went mostly to the front-end question, and I've got a real answer
now: not a winner, a *rule*. New app-like screens in Angular, existing pages enhanced
with Knockout, and stop trying to crown one framework king of everything.

The other thread was pushing work to where it belongs — window functions moving
reporting logic into SQL where it's a one-liner, feature flags letting unfinished
work ship dark, Grunt putting the front-end build in front-end tooling. A recurring
2012 lesson, still paying out.

And I started my first PCB, which is the resolution I'm most likely to actually
keep because it's the most fun. Breadboard to copper. February: route that board,
go deeper on Angular services, and figure out where this OWIN thing is heading.
