Big January. Signed the Lincoln Heights mortgage on the 8th, which is the thing I'll
remember this month for. The studio idea has been gestating since mid-2023 when I
started thinking seriously about a permanent space for the lab and the bench work — now
it has an address and a set of keys. The next few months will be slow buildout while I
keep the consulting work going.

On the tech side the year started with me actually using agent workflows on client
deliveries, not just recommending them. The pattern I'm settling into — bounded scope,
verifiable output — is holding up better than I expected. MCP is the protocol to
watch; it showed up quietly in November and I expect it to be everywhere by summer.
Instrumentation for LLM features is the gap most teams don't plan for until something
quietly goes wrong.

Local models on the new workstation are already paying rent — one client engagement
where the data can't leave their boundary, and the local inference is good enough that
the constraint stops being a hardship. The lab move starts in February when the bench
area wiring is done.
