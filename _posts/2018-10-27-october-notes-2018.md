October was the first month at City National Bank where I felt productive rather than
oriented. The ADR practice is now a team habit rather than a thing I'm advocating for,
and the API governance lint rules are catching real violations before design review
rather than during it. The Service Bus architecture decision is documented, approved,
and the first consumer service is in development.

The Angular 7 upgrade was a good checkpoint on the CDK virtual scrolling — we'd been
dragging around a third-party dependency that has a smaller maintenance team than the
Angular CDK now does, and that swap is a net reduction in risk for a codebase we'll
be running for years.

The pulse-oximeter PCBs arrived from the fab. The footprint changes I made in September
paid off — all the pads look correct on the board and the MAX30102 sits flush. Bring-up
is next weekend: apply solder paste, hot air, and see if the I2C address comes up on
the Pi. If it does I owe that unnamed Slack friend a coffee.
