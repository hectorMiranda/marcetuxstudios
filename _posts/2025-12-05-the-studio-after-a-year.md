Signed the mortgage January 8th. It's December and the studio has been fully operational
since March. Eleven months from "empty concrete room" to "this is where the work
happens," which is faster than I expected and more transformative than I expected.

The specific things that changed: I finish hardware projects now. The RF front-end board
that had been partially designed for two years was built and operational by July. The
CO2 sensors, the power monitor, the Altium migration — all of these happened in the
first year because the bench is set up to support the work instead of fighting it. The
friction reduction is real and measurable if you count completed projects.

The less measurable thing: I write better posts here. Not because the space is
inspiring in a literary sense, but because it's quiet and has a desk at the right height
and isn't also where I sleep and eat. The blog has a physical home. The posts in 2025
are the best in the archive, which I'll credit partly to having more to write about and
partly to having a real place to write it.
