Two weeks between jobs and no immediate deadline is a rarity. I'm using part of it to
do the reading that gets deferred when there's always another sprint, and part of it
to actually finish the ESP32 board rather than have it perpetually "almost done." The
KiCad design is at the fab. The reading list has been accumulating since August.

The role at SolidCommerce starts in January — marketplace integrations, which means
working with the Amazon, Walmart, and eBay seller APIs, managing product data across
multiple channels, and the reliability problems that come from depending on third-party
APIs you don't control. Different from a media pipeline but the same underlying
patterns: queue-driven workflows, event handling, idempotent processing. The domain
changes; the engineering problems rhyme.

I'm not sure what the stack looks like yet. The initial conversations mentioned .NET
and some Node, which is familiar territory. Whatever it is, I'll have opinions about
it by February. The December project is the ESP32 board, the holiday, and letting my
brain reset. It's the first December since 2013 where I'm not debugging something.
I intend to enjoy it.
