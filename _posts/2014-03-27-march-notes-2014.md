March had the feeling of a month where everything clicked a little better. Redis is
under sessions now and the second web server doesn't make things slower anymore. Angular
directives went from "the thing I was avoiding" to the thing I reach for first when
I copy-paste a component twice. The `ui-router` migration from February is holding up
— no regressions, and the nested views are genuinely better.

The microservices conversation keeps coming back. I spent part of a lunch reading the
Fowler piece carefully and the part I keep coming back to is the independently deployable
requirement. We're not there — not because the codebase is wrong but because the
deployment pipeline doesn't support it. That feels like the real bottleneck, not the
service boundaries.

At home, the PETG filament is dry and printing well. The next project is a filament
guide bracket that mounts to the Printrbot's frame — the spool falls off if I'm not
careful during long prints, which is maddening. Going to design it in Fusion 360 instead
of downloading someone else's. First time drawing something from scratch to print.
